Night Rider: A Night Interrupted — Coming Soon!

Introducing the Adventure

Night Rider Features

The upcoming title Night Rider: A Night Interrupted, from associates of the Elden Ring development team, is set in an explorable environment as large as Yellowstone National Park. Night Rider will support singleplayer and multiplayer in the seamless world where players will choose from up to 10 classes. With a distinctive exploration and combat system players will ride through an interrupted night.

Creative Genius

From the unhinged mind of Nebraska's video game bad boy, Ron Ronaldson, Night Rider: A Night Interrupted promises a thrilling experience like no other. Only a tortured genius from the streets of Lincoln could deliver on the on the fraught terrors of death and the American West. Merging uncanny purgatory with fatal finality join us with Ron Ronaldson and Night Rider through a 'A Night Interrupted.'

GOTY

Get ready for the game of the year! Night Rider: A Night Interrupted is set to redefine the genre with its stunning visuals and gripping gameplay. Many press reviewers are awarding Game of the Year nominations before the official release because of the era defining graphics and physics engine. Maintaining a motor vehicle in a videogame has never been more realistic and satisfying as with Night Rider's Night Bike.
